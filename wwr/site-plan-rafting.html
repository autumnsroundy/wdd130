Overview

Purpose

Bring people together through experiencing water rafting escapades. Water rafting is great for all ages and levels of experience, and through information and reviews people will be able to recognize the well rounded capability of rafting.

Audience

The audience for rafting refers to anyone seeking to fuel adventurous endeavors with friends and family. Anyone who might be undergoing a sudden need for wandering, or someone who is tired of being couped up in the city. All ages are encouraged to be apart of rafting excursions, and there is availability for trips for every level of experience. The audience will most likely visit a webiste through desktop or mobile device, seeing as how some of the audience members will be impulsive individuals. Search terms might include: Water rafting near me, or Best rafting in [set location].
